Effective project management is crucial for delivering software products within time and budget constraints while meeting customer requirements.

Gherkin Language and statements

Gherkin is a language used for writing Behavior-Driven Development (BDD) test cases. It uses a structured format with Given, When, Then statements to define the initial context, actions performed, and expected outcomes for each scenario. This makes test cases readable, reusable, and understandable to non-technical stakeholders.
